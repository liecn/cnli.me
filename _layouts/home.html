Biography

He is a graduate Student at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Michigan State University, working with Prof. Yunhao Liu and Prof. Zhichao Cao.

Prior to that, he received his BEng. in 2018 from School of Aerospace and Engineering, Tsinghua University, Beijing, China.

Research Interests

My research focuses on wireless networks and systems.
